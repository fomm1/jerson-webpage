Valorant

Info

Jett is the fastest of the agents, and only one that can levitate from boosted places without making any noise. She has 2 powerful smokes called Cloudburst, that can be instantly thrown in front of her.

Biography

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

Duelist

Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.

Cloudburst

Instantly throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. Hold the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair. Stays for 4.5 seconds. Makes a little noise when inside. Multiple clouds increase the volume of the clouds.

  • Charges: 3
  • Cost: 100

Skill 2 = "Q"

Updraft

Instantly propel Jett high into the air. Can be combined with the Tailwind.

  • Charges: 1
  • Cost: 100

Skill 3 = "E"

Tailwind

Instantly propel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she will propel forward. Can be combined with the Updraft.

  • Charges: 1
  • Cost: Free
